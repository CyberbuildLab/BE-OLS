BE-OLS follows a hybrid licensing model that separates code, metadata and curated datasets.
- Metadata extracted from ontologies is released under CC0. 
- BE OLS’s specific contributions-—e.g. harmonised categories, analyses, JSON/Excel datasets, and visualisations—-are licensed under CC BY 4.0 to ensure attribution 
- All BE-OLS's platform code is released under the Apache 2.0 licence. Documentation is licensed under CC BY 4.0. 
- The BE-OLS logo, name, and branding are not open licensed and remain protected.

Naturally, the referenced ontologies remain under their own licences.
